Ado

Ado is an utaite who began her activities on January 10, 2017 at the age of 14. In late 2020, she exploded onto the music scene with her debut release of "USSEEWA" Nvat just 17 years old. As of Sep 2023, it has over 296 million views on YouTube, and over 6.5 million plays with over twenty-five thousand mylists on Niconico. Since then, Ado has been garnering lots of attention when releasing new covers or original music and she also releases albums of her covered songs as well.

Ado has a versatile voice and is able to add power to it. Her vocals, seeming somewhat mature for her age, usually give off an angsty vibe, as of a rebellious teenager especially in some of her original and cover songs like "Show" and "Crime & Punishment," where she best utilizes it.

She uses growls to inject a gravelly angst into her voice but can also belt a soft and clear falsetto on a dime. Not only that, her vocal range itself aside from her techniques is astounding; displayed in "God-ish" to its fullest extent, Ado's latest measure of vocal range is from a low and rumbling fried B2 banging all the way up through to an ear-piercing screaming C7.

TUYU

A music unit consisting of Pusu on guitar and in charge of composing the songs, Rei as vocals, and miro on piano. The group was formed on June 12, 2019. Its name comes from the Japanese word for “rainy season” during which it was formed. Currently has over 1,100,000 YouTube subscribers. In addition to its showpiece titled “Compared Child” (tr. the girl who gets compared) that has had over 43million views, every music video that they’ve uploaded have been played over a million times. They’re constantly ranked in both Japanese and foreign music distribution sites and appear in their popular playlists. A group of artists to keep an eye on with many fans around the world.

FAKE TYPE.

FAKE TYPE. is a music duo formed in Tokyo, Japan in 2013 consisting of rapper TOPHAMHAT-KYO and trackmaker DYES IWASAKI.[1] They are known for having composed the main theme for Goonya Monster "GO ON YA WAY", which features CHOGAKUSEI. A collab with Goonya Monster was announced alongside SAKANAMON's on October 18, 2022 and finally released on December 8.[2] The collab included a DLC that adds "GO ON YA WAY" (and an instrumental version) as background tracks, a DLC that adds TOPHAMHAT-KYO and DYES IWASAKI as playable monsters and clothing items related to both FAKE TYPE. and CHOGAKUSEI.

jon-Yakitory

He wrote, composed and arranged the song Koko de Ikiteru which appeared in the opening credits of the anime television series The Detective Is Already Dead. can be heard and was sung by Mary. The song was initially released digitally. In August 2021, the single was released physically, along with the end credits Kodō and the soundtrack to the series. The soundtrack reached 96th and 269th place in the Japanese album charts. Jon-Yakitory released the song Rasen with the Japanese singer Ado , which was used in a commercial for the video game Lost Judgment .
